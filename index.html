About Us

About Us

The Parks (Ilford) Management Company is a Residents’ Management Company (RMC) that has been formed to protect the interests of leaseholders of the private residential estate, The Parks (Ilford). The company consists of members(Leaseholders and freeholders). There are 212 members. consisting of 57 houses, 103 flats and 52 social housing flats.
Read More on Companies House Website

The RMC is responsible for the management and maintenance of the buildings/estate, as well as compliance with the obligations of the lease and a range of statutory requirements (subject to the lease structure). The directors of the estate, who are unpaid and appointed by the members, decide how best to achieve this by appointing a professional managing agent.


Managing Agent

The managing agent is a company that is appointed by the directors to manage the estate. They manage leases, transfers, contracts and general upkeep and repair of the estate. On the direction of the directors, they arrange insurance, contracts for cleaning and ground maitenance and other day to day activities for repairs.

Bulk Waste

Bulk Waste

The Parks (Ilford) is a private residential estate. This means council do not pick up the bulk refusal items like Fridge, Furniture, Sofa and Beds. It is illegal to dump the bulk rubbish and it costs money to all of us as we arrange private contractors to pick them up. In financial year 2018 and 2019, we paid in access of £30,000 each year. Hopefully once the CCTV cameras are operational, we will start charging the residence who found to be dumping the bulk items.

How to arrange Bulk Waste:

Redbridge council allows residents to arrange the large item collection for free Click Here . You can also arrange the pickup with Heart Foundation (Click Here) or find a Bulk Waste Company online. Please follow these steps:
  1. Book the collection with the council/Heart Foundation/Private.
  2. A night before the collection date, place the item outside the house or near bin area (if live in flats)
  3. Make sure proper notices are affixed on the bulk waste items. Affix the collection confirmation letter/details as well as your address so everyone is aware that it will be collected by council.
Please make sure you don’t put the items outside before the collection date and always post the confirmation document/property address so everyone is aware of it.
